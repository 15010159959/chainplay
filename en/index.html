    Melo the Converter

    A long time ago, on a mysterious and wonderful planet, there was a magical animal that could turn a pearl into a lot of pearls. People called it a beast, but he only had 24 hours of life. In order to continue his life, everyone kept feeding. Its pearls continue life and Melo the Converter to give back pearls to people. If the conversion animal spends 24 lives, it will dedicate all the pearls it has collected to the last person who continues its life.

    1、Melo the Converter is an application based on the nebulas chain. It needs to rely on the nebula chrome wallet plugin. The installation address is:Chrome Web Store

    2、The converted beast has only 24 hours of life and can continue its life by buying pearls. Whenever you buy a pearl, the time will increase by 30s, and when it reaches 24 hours, it will not increase.

    3、Your purchase of pearls not only continues the life of the Melo the Converter, you will also receive a bonus bonus for the Melo the Converter.

    4、If you are lucky enough, you will get all the pearls that the converted beast collects with the other 6 people.

    5、Pearl will grow slowly as the number of purchases increases.
